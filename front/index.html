About This Project

This tool reads medical PDF documents and summarizes events like diagnoses, treatment dates, and the attending doctors or hospitals. It filters out noise like opinions, symptoms, and legal boilerplate.

Key Features

PDF to Timeline

Automatically extract and structure medical events from scanned or digital PDF documents.

Intelligent Filtering

Removes irrelevant information like opinions or vague statements, giving you only what matters.

Export-Ready Summaries

Summaries are clean and structured — ready for legal, insurance, or administrative use.
